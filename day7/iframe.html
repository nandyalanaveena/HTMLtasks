VEG AND NON VEG STARTERS

Veg Starters:

  • French Fries

    French Fries are one of the most popular snacks worldwide, known for their crispy exterior and fluffy interior. They are made by deep-frying thinly sliced potatoes and are typically served as a side dish, snack, or fast-food item. See More...

  • Paneer Tikka

    Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian dish made from marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes that are grilled or roasted. It is a beloved appetizer, especially in North Indian cuisine, and is often served at parties, restaurants, or as a starter in meals. See More...

  • Veg Manchurian

    Veg Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that combines Indian flavors with Chinese cooking techniques. It is a favorite among vegetarians and a staple in many Indian-Chinese restaurants. See More...

  • Dahi Puri

    Dahi Puri is a popular Indian street food that belongs to the chaat family. It is a delicious and tangy snack made with crispy puris (small, hollow, fried dough balls) filled with yogurt (dahi), chutneys, and various toppings. It's a delightful explosion of flavors and textures in every bite! See More...


Non Veg Starters:

  • Chicken Lollipop

    Chicken Lollipop is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer made by marinating chicken drumettes or wings in flavorful spices and frying them until crisp. The unique presentation, resembling a "lollipop," makes it a favorite at parties and restaurants. See More...

  • Chicken 65

    Chicken 65 is a spicy and flavorful South Indian appetizer made with marinated, deep-fried chicken pieces. Its origin is traced to Tamil Nadu, with several theories explaining its unique name. It is known for its fiery flavor, crisp texture, and bold seasoning, making it a favorite across India. See More...

  • Fish Fry

    Fish Fry is a popular dish across various cuisines, especially in coastal regions. It typically involves marinating fish with spices and frying it until crispy. The preparation methods and spices vary by culture, but the result is a flavorful, tender fish with a crispy exterior. See More...

  • Chicken Tikka

    Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with boneless chicken pieces marinated in a mix of yogurt and spices, then grilled or baked to perfection. The dish is renowned for its smoky, tangy, and slightly spicy flavor. See More...

  • Strawberry
